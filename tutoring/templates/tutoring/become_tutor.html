Tutor Terms & Conditions
1. Tutor Responsibilities

As a tutor, you agree to:

  • Provide accurate information about your qualifications
  • Maintain professional conduct with students
  • Be punctual for scheduled sessions
  • Provide quality tutoring services
  • Respect student privacy and confidentiality
2. Payment Terms
  • You set your own hourly rate
  • Payments are processed through the platform
  • Platform fee: 15% of each session
  • Payments are released 24 hours after session completion
3. Code of Conduct
  • No discrimination based on race, gender, religion, etc.
  • No inappropriate behavior or communication
  • Academic integrity must be maintained
  • No completion of assignments for students
4. Platform Rules
  • Keep communication on the platform for safety
  • Report any issues or concerns immediately
  • Maintain a minimum 4.0 rating to remain active
  • Respond to booking requests within 24 hours
